How ClaimXpert Works

1. Register & Log In

Insurance providers securely log in to manage and view client-submitted claims.

2. Review Claims

Access uploaded documents like medical bills and treatment reports for quick verification.

3. Verify & Update

Verify claim authenticity and update claim status: Processing, Approved, or Rejected.

4. Notify Clients

Clients get real-time updates on their claim status directly through the app.
